Evesdropping and phone tapping is not just the problem of America. This culture is spreading fast. Every government wants to spy on its citizen in the name of homeland security. This is all fine if it is practised on select suspects but becomes a real problem when spying is done on the entire population without convincing reasons. Hence it is very important to equip ourselves with technology to protect ourselves from this intrusion.

BindaasTalk helps you to protect your communication by encrypting the voice data before it is passed on to the telecom operator. It can be easily installed on most smartphones (running iOS or Andriod; other platforms like Blackberry will be supported later). As a one time configuration, you'll be asked to use a password for encrypting your voice. The same password will be requried by the decoding party to listen to the call. This way, if the call is tapped, the data is undecipherable without the password.

How does it work?
It's an app running on your phone which actively encrpts the outgoing voice signals.
